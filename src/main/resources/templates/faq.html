How does ShoutX work?

ShoutX connects creators who want to grow their audience. You can request shoutouts from other creators or accept requests from them. Both parties have 24 hours to post, ensuring fairness and accountability.

What happens if I don't post within 24 hours?

If you fail to post within 24 hours, a violation is recorded. 3 violations result in a permanent account ban. We take accountability seriously to ensure a trustworthy community.

How does the rating system work?

After each successful exchange, both creators can rate each other from 1-5 stars. Your average rating builds your reputation on the platform, helping other creators decide to work with you.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept multiple payment methods: Razorpay (credit/debit cards), PayPal, UPI, and Paytm. All payments are secure and encrypted.

Is my account safe?

Yes! We use OAuth2 authentication with Instagram, so your password never reaches our servers. All data is encrypted and we follow industry best practices for security.
