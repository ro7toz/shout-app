Data Deletion Request

Last updated: December 2025

Delete Your Account & Data

We respect your privacy and your right to delete your personal information. If you wish to delete your account and all associated data from Shout, please follow the instructions below.

How to Request Data Deletion

Option 1: Via Email (Recommended)

Send an email to tushkinit@gmail.com with the subject "Data Deletion Request" and include:

  • Your Shout username
  • Your email address associated with the account
  • Your Facebook/Instagram user ID
  • A brief statement: "I request to delete my account and all personal data from Shout"

Option 2: In-App Request

Log into your account and go to Settings â†’ Privacy â†’ Request Data Deletion.

What Data Gets Deleted

When you request data deletion, we will delete:

  • Your account profile and login credentials
  • Your personal information (name, email, profile picture)
  • All shoutout requests you created
  • All ratings and reviews you submitted
  • All messages and communications
  • Your activity logs and browsing history on our app
  • Any connected social media information

What Data May Be Retained

For legal and business purposes, we may retain:

  • Anonymized usage data and analytics (cannot identify you)
  • Records required by law (taxation, legal compliance)
  • Backup copies (which will be deleted after 90 days)

Processing Time

Your data deletion request will be processed within 30 days. You will receive a confirmation email once your account and data have been deleted.

What Happens After Deletion

After your account is deleted:

  • You will no longer be able to log in
  • Your profile will no longer be visible to other users
  • Your username may be available for others to use
  • Any public information you shared may remain in screenshots or external sources
  • You can request to recreate your account after 30 days

For Facebook Users

If you logged in using Facebook, deleting your Shout account will NOT delete your Facebook account. You can revoke Shout's access to your Facebook data at any time in your Facebook Settings â†’ Apps and Websites.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I undo data deletion?

A: No, once your data is deleted, it cannot be recovered. However, you can recreate your account after 30 days.

Q: Will my shoutout history be deleted?

A: Yes, all shoutout requests, ratings, and exchange history will be deleted. Other users will see "Deleted User" for any exchanges involving you.

Q: How do I know my data was deleted?

A: We will send you a confirmation email at your registered email address when the deletion is complete.

Q: Can I request data deletion through Facebook?

A: Yes, if you request data deletion through Facebook's Data Deletion Tool, we will process it within 30 days.

Your Privacy Rights

Under applicable data protection laws (including GDPR and India's Personal Data Protection Act), you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your data (Right to be Forgotten)
  • Restrict processing of your data
  • Data portability (receive your data in a structured format)
  • Lodge a complaint with a data protection authority
